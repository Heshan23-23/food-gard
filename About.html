ABOUT US

Come, eat the best food on earth

Welcome to Food Gard, where culinary excellence meets a warm, inviting ambiance. Nestled in the heart of Colombo, our story is one of passion and dedication to the art of fine dining.

Established in 2025, we've been serving up delectable dishes that are a harmonious blend of flavors, using the freshest locally-sourced ingredients. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where guests can savor exquisite food and make lasting memories.

With a team of skilled chefs, attentive servers, and a commitment to unparalleled customer service, we strive to exceed your expectations with every visit. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or seeking a delightful meal, we invite you to experience the essence of Food Gard — where taste, quality, and hospitality come together to create an unforgettable dining experience.

Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to sharing our passion for food with you.
